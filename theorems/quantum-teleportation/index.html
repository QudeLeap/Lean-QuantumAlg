Quantum teleportation

Theorem statement

Suppose Alice has an $n$-qubit state $\ket{\psi}$ and Alice and Bob share $n$ Bell states. There exists a quantum protocol using $2n$ classical bits to transfer $\ket{\psi}$ to Bob locally.
